Meta Content Scheduler – Full Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Instagram Reels Automation Agent for Maldives Resorts
1. Product Overview
Meta Content Scheduler is an AI-powered automation platform that discovers, evaluates, and schedules high-quality Instagram Reels sourced exclusively from official Maldives resort Instagram accounts. The system posts 5 optimized Reels per day while maintaining Meta compliance, brand safety, and performance consistency.
2. Goals & Objectives
• Automate daily Instagram Reels publishing
• Maintain premium content quality
• Eliminate manual curation
• Avoid Meta account penalties
• Increase organic reach and enquiry intent

3. Target Users (B2C Focused)
•Primary Users (B2C – Demand Side)

Top international travelers interested in Maldives vacations, especially from:

-	United Kingdom
-	Canada
-	China
-	Russia
-	Europe (France, Germany, Spain, Bulgaria etc)
-	Middle East (UAE, Egypt, Qatar, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia)

These users consume aspirational, viral Instagram Reels and are driven by visual storytelling, luxury experiences, and short-form video inspiration.

• Secondary Users (B2C Distribution & Sales)

-	Travel agents selling Maldives holiday packages directly to consumers
-	Tour operators focused on outbound Maldives travel

• Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) (e.g., AtollDiscovery.com)

These users leverage the platform to consistently publish high-performing resort content that converts interest into enquiries and bookings.

•Supporting Users

-	Resort marketing teams (content source & amplification)
-	Social media managers handling consumer-facing travel brands

4. Success Metrics (KPIs)
